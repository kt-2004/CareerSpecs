Hotel management involves overseeing the day-to-day operations of hotels, resorts, and other lodging establishments to ensure a seamless guest experience. Biotechnology

Biotechnology is a rapidly advancing field at the intersection of biology and technology, aimed at harnessing living organisms and biological systems to develop innovative products and solutions. It encompasses a wide range of applications, including genetic engineering, pharmaceuticals, agriculture, environmental remediation, and industrial processes.
